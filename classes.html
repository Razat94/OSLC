Homework Club

Mon – Fri: 3p - 7p

One Step Learning Center provides a comfortable and fitting work environment for elementary students to complete their homework and school projects. Our organized and caring staff of tutors work with these students to encourage independent work habits and self-starting attitudes, while also monitoring their progress and workload to ensure academic success.

Gifted Education

Mon – Fri: 3p - 7p

Gifted education is reserved for students who can work at a faster pace as we try to challenge their critical thinking skills through extended education classes. Gifted students are required to complete all school homework and projects in a timely manner in order to allow time for their enrichment schedule workload. For certain students who excel in this program, we offer additional services under the title "Highly Gifted," which includes an invitation to participate in private tutoring sessions to help with scholastic testing such as ISEE, HSPT, and GATE.

SAT/ACT/AP & College Prep

Mon – Sun: Time Varies

For our older students, we offer prep courses for all of the challenges that await them in high school. This includes SAT prep, ACT prep, AP subject tutoring, and college admissions counseling. As students begin the process for completing college applications, we assist them by monitoring the entire process with organized outlines to help guide the student through this stressful process.

Private Tutoring

Mon – Sun: Time Varies

We welcome all students to join us for a private tutoring session! Your student may need reoccurring sessions to assist with a challenging class, a one-on-one session to tackle an upcoming test, or just a motivating session to assess the student's current status to teach new study techniques and test taking skills. Whichever one it may be, One Step Learning Center is here for you.

Math Group Sessions

Mon – Fri: Time Varies

After assessing your student's math level, we place them in a group session that will challenge and support their current math education. Our goal is to prepare the student for math concepts that they will encounter in the future so that we can ensure math readiness.

Writing Group Sessions

Mon – Fri: Time Varies

Writing is such an important skill since we use it in all aspects of learning. Our writing groups focus on reading comprehension, grammar skills, and preparing for the writing styles that your student will be required to do during the school year. Their level placement is dependent on grade and skill, and our goal is to help develop competent readers and writers so that learning because fun and enjoyable.
